Self Care Club

a support group that never meets, created by a designer with a serotonin deficiency
Self Care Club is a lifestyle brand dedicated to initiating a conversation around the triumphs and hardships of mental illness. The central theme brings to light what remains invisible and more than not unspoken. It emphasizes the importance to unite under a common goal of ending the stigma. It's okay not to be okay.
