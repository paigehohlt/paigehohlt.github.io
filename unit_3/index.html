The purpose behind this shop is simple — to give a voice to the struggles in our brain. The goal is to start a conversation around the hardships and triumphs of mental health and sourcing help from others who know exactly how you feel. Whether it’s for you or a loved one, all products represented on this site give a foundation for people to connect, lend a helping hand, and laugh a little.
